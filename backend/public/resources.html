Prepare and Stay Safe

Access safety tips and emergency guides to protect yourself and your community from floods, wildfires, storms, and more.

Safety Tips

Flood Safety

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground if flooding is imminent.
  • Keep emergency supplies like food, water, and flashlights ready.

Wildfire Safety

  • Create a defensible space around your home by clearing dry vegetation.
  • Have an evacuation plan and practice it with your family.
  • Stay informed through local news and alerts.

Storm Safety

  • Secure outdoor items that could become projectiles in high winds.
  • Stay indoors and away from windows during a storm.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials like blankets and first aid.
